Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a GoCardless account?
Yes. This integration connects your existing GoCardless account to GoHighLevel. If you don't have one, you can sign up at gocardless.com. They offer a sandbox environment for testing.
Which payment methods are supported?
GoCardless supports UK BACS Direct Debit, SEPA Direct Debit (Euro), and ACH (US). The specific methods available depend on your GoCardless account configuration and customer location.
How long do payments take?
BACS (UK): 3 working days to confirm, 4-5 days to receive funds. SEPA (Euro): 3-4 days. The integration updates payment status in real-time via webhooks as each stage completes.
Is my data secure?
Yes. All API tokens are encrypted at rest. We never store full bank account numbers â€” that data stays with GoCardless (who are FCA-regulated and PCI-DSS compliant). Communication between systems uses HTTPS/TLS.
Can I test before going live?
Absolutely. Connect your GoCardless sandbox account first. Test mandates, payments, and webhooks with fake data. When you're ready, switch to your live GoCardless token â€” no reinstallation needed.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. Cancel your subscription at any time. Your access continues until the end of the billing period. Existing GoCardless mandates and payments are unaffected â€” they live in your GoCardless account.
